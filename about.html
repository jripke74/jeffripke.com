About

I live in Spokane, WA with my wife Heidi and two of our kids. I have a third child but he is currently serving in the US Marines. I spend my free time learning to program or outdoors working on maintaining our five acres of trees or working in the garden. I also spend allot of time as a Royal Ranger Commander with our youngest son.

I am currently in the process of learning to code and currently I am focusing on iOS development. I have been interested in computers since my high school days. My first computer system was an Amiga 2500. I love technology and learning about it, it is a big part of my life.

I have been working in the communications field for the last 18 years, in the Navy and currently at ATT. Working on dozens of different systems both maintaining and operating. I have done everything from installing equipment to updating software on dozens of different systems. I also work on networking equipment everything from changing cards to running fibers. I also maintain power systems including UPS batteries, ATS (Automatic Transfer Switches), generators and HVAC systems.
